Graphic Image #1

Subheading

This image uses a public domain image from nasa as a background and layers a dall over it. drown ontop of the dall with a photoshop brush is a pentegram and using a seperate brush on the same layer are "blood trails". This whole layer has an opacity applied to it to blend better as well as a gradiant funning from top (lighter red) to bottom (darker red). The eye whites of the dall have been cut to reveal the background. Finnally, a butterfly is finally layered ontop of all of this.

Subheading

This image has 4 layers. The background, the man, the butterflys, and finnally the mans lip. The lip is the last layer to make the illusion that the man is ingesting the butterfly. additionally, the butterflies are warped to apear to be at different angles (perspectives) then they were in the original images.

Graphic Image #3

Subheading

This image has a couple layers of the original background plus a new background color and a the head is cropped out and turned. There is excessive use of the spot healing and healing brush to get rid of ertifacts but also to help get rid of the old background under where the head used to be. Finally, the eyes are on a seperate layer with a slight transparency to them and the retinas are redrawn with the brush tool.

Graphic Image #4

Subheading

This image is origannally a series of layers that filtered the background (originally grayscale) to be colorful. Each color is a layer and each color is overlayed over a diffent parrelel set of walls. Finaaly, a gradiant is overlayed over the entire thing. The original purpose was to choose overlay settings that could only bland the gradiant onto present layers. After turning off the background, the image looked more interesting so it was kept that way.

3D Image #1

Subheading

This graphic is somewhat self explanuitory. The panguin killed the whale and the girl looked on in harror. the panguin felt so cool he held his pose for a while while the girl mornes over the whale in disbelief.
